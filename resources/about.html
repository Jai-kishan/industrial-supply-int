Who we are

Industrial Supply International – Powering Global Industry!

Industrial Supply International is a trusted supplier and exporter of high-performance industrial equipment, spare parts, tools, consumables, structural steel, and heavy machinery. With a strong foundation in Australia, the company serves as a vital link in the supply chain for the oil & gas and mining sectors across the Pacific region, the Middle East, and Africa.

  • Industrial Equipment & Machinery: From drilling rigs to processing units, from open pit to underground mine, we deliver robust solutions tailored to demanding environments.
  • Spare Parts & Tools: Precision-engineered components and tools that ensure operational continuity and safety.
  • Consumables & Structural Steel: High-grade materials for construction, maintenance, and production needs.
